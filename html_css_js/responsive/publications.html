Aims and Scope

The Archives of Reconstructive Microsurgery (Arch Reconstr Microsurg, ARMS) is the official publication of the Korean Society for Microsurgery and launched in 1981. This journal is published twice a year in May 30th and November 30th. From 2013, ARMS continues Journal of the Korean Society for Microsurgery, which was first published in 1992. In November 2013, the journal was renamed "Archives of Reconstructive Microsurgery" (print ISSN 1226-2706, online ISSN 2288-6184; now print ISSN 2383-5257, online ISSN 2288-6184 from November 2014) by Korean Society for Microsurgery. This journal is indexed in KoreaMed, KoMCI, DOI/CrossRef, Google Scholar, and EBSCO.
